features of SBOX

S-BOX is a RFID scanner rigged mahogany box that can scan cards and instantly play songs from Spotify through an external speaker.





we loved the potential that RFID could express, so we wanted to make sure it would respond as fast and as efficiently so that no waiting was involved in our process
